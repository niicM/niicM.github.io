Project Euler: Problem 26

I used an algorithm that does what I would do had I had to do it by hand only in Clojure. I wonder if there is any flaw. 

(defn period-length [b]
"Calculates the lenth of the period when dividing 1 by b"
(loop [rems #{10}, next-a 10]
  (let [next-rem (* 10 (rem next-a b))]
    (if (rems next-rem)
      (count rems)
      (recur (conj rems next-rem) next-rem)))))
